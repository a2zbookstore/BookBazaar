## 🚀 What You Need to Do Next (30 Minutes)

### Step 1: Create Google Merchant Center Account (10 min)
1. Go to: https://merchants.google.com/
2. Click "Get Started"
3. Sign in with your Google account
4. Enter business info:
   - Business name: **A2Z BOOKSHOP**
   - Country: **India**
   - Time zone: **Asia/Kolkata**

### Step 2: Verify Website (5 min)
- Choose verification method:
  - **Easiest**: Use Google Search Console (if already verified) ✅
  - Or: HTML meta tag (already on your site)

### Step 3: Create Product Feed (10 min)
1. In Merchant Center, go to **Products > Feeds**
2. Click **"+"** to create new feed
3. Select:
   - Country: **India**
   - Language: **English**
   - Destinations: ✅ **Shopping ads** and ✅ **Free listings**
4. Click "Continue"
5. Feed name: `A2Z Bookshop Main Feed`
6. Input method: **Scheduled fetch**
7. File URL: `https://a2zbookshop.com/product-feed.xml`
8. Fetch frequency: **Daily**
9. Time: **2:00 AM** (or any off-peak time)
10. Click **"Create Feed"**

### Step 4: Configure Settings (5 min)
1. **Shipping**: Go to Tools > Shipping and returns
   - Add shipping rates for India and worldwide
2. **Return Policy**: Add your return policy (30 days recommended)
3. **Business Info**: Add customer service email and phone

### Step 5: Enable Free Listings (2 min)
1. Go to **Growth > Manage programs**
2. Find **"Surfaces across Google"**
3. Click **"Enable"**

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Feed Not Uploading?
- Test URL: https://a2zbookshop.com/product-feed.xml
- Should show XML, not HTML

### Products Disapproved?
- Check Merchant Center diagnostics
- Common issues: Missing ISBN, poor image quality, price format

### No Results After 4 Weeks?
- Ensure feed status is "Active" in Merchant Center
- Check for errors in Products > Diagnostics
- Verify shipping and return policies are configured
